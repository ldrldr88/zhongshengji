Why Sheng Ji Requires Two Days

Day 1: Purification, Empowerment & Preparation at the Studio

Day 1 takes place at Master Lin's ritual studio. The purpose is to cleanse the client's fate chart of accumulated negative energies, empower the ceremonial box with ritual authority, and prepare all items for the dragon vein interment on Day 2. This foundation is essential — without it, the earth qi of the dragon vein cannot properly bond with the client.

Day 2: Interment at the Dragon Vein Site

Day 2 takes place at the Fu Gui Shuang Quan dragon vein plot in Taiwan. The ceremonial box is formally buried in the earth, creating a permanent energetic bond between the client and the dragon vein's qi. From this moment, the earth qi continuously influences the client's destiny.

Day 1: Full Ritual Sequence (8 Steps)

Steps 1–3: Opening Prayers, Petition Document & Disaster Dispelling

The ceremony opens with prayers inviting Taoist protective deities to witness and guard the ritual. A formal petition document (shu wen) detailing the client's name, birth data and intentions is then presented to the heavenly realm. The disaster-dispelling sequence uses a symbolic stand-in (ti shen) to absorb and remove accumulated misfortunes and negative energies from the client's fate chart.

Steps 4–6: Seven-Star Lamp, Wealth Replenishment & Empowerment

The Seven-Star Lamp (qi xing deng), representing the Big Dipper, is lit to stabilise the client's primordial spirit and neutralise hidden negative fate energies. The wealth-replenishment ritual invites the God of Wealth to restore the client's financial and karmic fortune reserves. The ceremonial box contents are then formally empowered through the opening-of-eyes rite (kai guang dian yan), activating the items as living energetic proxies for the client.

Step 7–8: Paper Effigy Placement & Day 1 Completion

A personalised paper effigy bearing the client's full name and birth data is prepared and placed — this serves as the client's symbolic representative within the dragon vein. Day 1 concludes, with all items ready for the Day 2 interment ceremony.

Day 2: Dragon Vein Site Ceremony (7 Steps)

Steps 1–3: Arrival, Prayers & Auspicious Water Ritual

The team arrives at the Fu Gui Shuang Quan dragon vein plot. Prayers are offered specifically to the local dragon deity to formally introduce the client and request acceptance. At the auspicious hour selected based on the client's BaZi, the Dragon Deity Wealth Water ritual imbues the ceremonial box with the concentrated essence of the dragon vein.

Steps 4–6: Five-Direction Guardian Invocation & Core Interment

Guardians of the five directions (east, south, west, north, centre) are summoned to form a protective boundary around the burial site. The wealth-mother (qian mu) token is placed inside the ceremonial box. The core step: the ceremonial box is formally interred in the dragon vein plot — the client's destiny is now permanently bonded with the earth qi.

Step 7: Ceremony Completion & Boundary Sealing

The five-direction guardians are formally dismissed and the protective boundary is sealed. The ceremony is complete. The full HD video recording is prepared for delivery to the client along with the dragon-qi garment and silver ingot.
